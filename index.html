How to play

Gather 20 waterdrops until time runs out in order to extinguish the fire.

Avoid clicking on firedrops as they will hurt you and take away one life every time. Remember: the firedrops are also dangerous after reaching the floor.

You have three lives to begin with.

If you lose a life, you can get another one by clicking on a heart falling down.

Note: You can access pause, restart, exit, and mute buttons by clicking on the Settings icon.

The game was made as a study project for the Basic Animation Theme, Multimedia Design Program at KEA, 2020.
